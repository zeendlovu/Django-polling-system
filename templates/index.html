Boost Participation

Choosing online voting for your election will more likely boost the participation. You will give the chance to members that otherwise may not be able to vote.

Less Physical infastructure required

Online voting leads to the avoidance of the need for all the physical infrastructure usually required on a traditional voting. No need of paper, printing, physical urns or staff may, therefore, lead to a lower monetary investment.

Accurate, Fast and Easy Voting

Since the tally in online voting is run by machines, you can assure that it will not have human counting errors and that it will in most cases run faster than a count carried out by persons, so the results of your election will be available sooner.

Troubleshooting Questions

These are some of the potential troubleshooting questions which may arise.

  1. To register, click on the registration link on the navigation bar
  2. After registration, the site will automatically redirect you to the login page
  3. Registration Number and a secure password are required
  4. Password cannot be less than 8 characters
  5. For enhanced security, users are encougared to come up with passwords containing a mix of alphanumeric characters and symbols

  1. This Voting Platform is only limited to current UZ students and staff ONLY
  2. Registration Number is compulsory to check if you are a current UZ student or not
  3. Coverage will be nationwide, outside Zimbabwe access will be very limited

  1. Results are published on the same day elections took place or next day in the morning
  2. In order to access results, users have to be logged in
  3. After current election, user accounts will cease operation till next next election.
